Art Profile

Title: Be Brave

In my art, I like to make silhouette art. I wanted to make it more dynamic since there isn't quite much detail, but I wanted to challenge myself to make something unique.

I made this art piece in March 2016 as part of my AP Art Portfolio. And what I was inspired to make this piece was my sense of adventure into dreams. We don't know what we are really dreaming in the beginning, but we are able to remember some parts when we are nearing the end of our slumber. Far out in the distant stars, there we dream on a blue world of spirit and memories. I want to make this kind of video game with a lot of twists and turns.

I have created more artwork on DeviantArt. Some of it is experimental and practice drawing. I use traditional and digital technique for some of my drawings.
