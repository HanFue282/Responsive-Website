About Me



My name is Hannah Joy Fuentes. I am 21 years-old, and I am pursuring my future career in video game development. I'm still attending college, and I self-teach drawing anatomy, photoshop, and writing. I'm currently having a artist block, but I have been seeking help and it's a work in progress.

I've dreamed of becoming a video game developer since I was 13 years-old. Video games have brought me joy and pain at times, but I embrace it all and not regret it because my life has it's rollercoaster of adventures.

Currently attending the SMU Bootcamp is bringing me confidence towards my future career. I feel the drive to be better and the drive to do hardwork. I can't wait to learn more about the coding program and use it for my future.
